My Journey into Tech

From Curiosity to Coding

Meet Ivy a woman who recently decided to embark on a new and exicting journey into the world of technology and coding. She has been curious about how websites and applications
worked,but never imagined she would start learning how to build them herself.

The First Steps

Ivy began by learning the basics of HTML,the foundation of web development . She created her first web page, adding headings, paragraphs and even formatting her text using bold , italic and underline elements

Every new tag she learned brought a sense of accomplishment

She quickly became comfortable with HTML elements and is eager to explore more

Next on her list : learning more HTML elements and CSS to style her web pages

Challenges and Growth

The journey was`nt easy. Ivy encountered errors ,bugs and chalenges that tested her patience . But she never gave up. She knew that with every mistake,she was step closer to becoming a
confident coder.

At the end of this program, Ivy can build simple web pages and will slowly venture into Javascript to make her projects interactive.

The Road Ahead

Ivy knows that the road to mastering tech is long,but she is excited for the future. She`s even started sharing her progress on social media to inspire others. especially other women ,to dive into the world of coding .

Conclusion

Ivy`s journey is just beginning . With passion, determination, and the ability to never stop learning . she knows that the possibilities in tech are endless.
